## Audience
General public. Casual Instagram scrollers curious about AI but not deep into tech. Copy is plain-English, no jargon.

## Hook Strategy
Countdown format (#7 to #1). Suspense drives swipe-through. Most impactful update for general public (#1 Search) held for last. CTA slide blends follower growth + comment engagement.
